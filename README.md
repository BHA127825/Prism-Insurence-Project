# Prism-Insurence-Project
🚀 Insurance Data Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project
I’m pleased to share my latest Power BI project focused on Insurance Data Analysis and Business Insights Generation.
In this project, I worked with raw insurance policy and claims data and transformed it into a fully interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders understand performance, risk exposure, and claim patterns in a clear and structured way.
📊 Key Highlights of the Dashboard:
• Total Premium Amount, Coverage Amount & Claim Amount overview
 • Policy distribution by type
 • Active vs Inactive policies analysis
 • Claim status breakdown (Pending, Rejected, Settled)
 • Claim amount analysis by age group
 • Gender-wise policy distribution
 • KPI cards for quick business insights
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
 ✔ Monitor insurance performance
 ✔ Analyze risk exposure
 ✔ Track claim trends
 ✔ Make data-driven business decisions
Through this project, I strengthened my skills in data visualization, business intelligence, and analytical thinking.
